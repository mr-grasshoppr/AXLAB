About

We believe in learning by doing

Art Experience Lab (AXLab) takes a participatory approach to research and design. We ground our work in learning sciences and human-centered design methods, helping teams move from discussion to action through rapid prototyping, making, and experimentation.

Our practice bridges academic research with real-world application, exploring how AI transforms creative work and how new practices emerge through hands-on experimentation.
