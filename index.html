Research and content strategy for a collaboration exploring how AI image generation can inform industrial design practices. Working with Google DeepMind, Ross Lovegrove, and Modem, we experimented with how AI-generated concepts translated into physical furniture prototypes. Results shaped DeepMind's product strategy for image generation.
