About

We believe in learning by doing

Art Experience Lab takes a rigorous, participatory approach to research and design. We ground our work in learning sciences and arts-based methods, helping teams move from discussion to action through prototyping, making, and experimentation.

Our practice bridges academic research with practical application, building communities of practice across sectors and disciplines. We're particularly focused on how AI transforms creative work and how people learn through hands-on engagement with emerging technologies.

Art Experience Lab is led by Maryanna Rogers, a researcher and designer with a PhD in Learning Sciences from Stanford. The practice draws on experience from Google DeepMind, Meta, Mozilla, and The Tech Interactive to bridge academic research with applied design work. Learn more about the founder →
